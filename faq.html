FAQ

+ —

What are the gym's operating hours?

Our gym is open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekdays and from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekends.

+ —

Do I need to book a session to use the gym?

No, you don’t need to book a session for general use. However, booking is required for personal training or group classes.

+ —

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available. You can book sessions directly at the front desk or through our website.

+ —

Is there a free trial for new members?

Absolutely! We offer a 7-day free trial for all new members. Visit the front desk to activate your trial.

+ —

What types of membership plans are available?

We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual membership plans. Discounts are available for long-term plans and student memberships.

+ —

Are there group classes, and how do I join?

Yes, we offer a variety of group classes such as yoga, spinning, and HIIT. You can sign up through our website or at the reception desk.

+ —

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot for our members, free of charge.

+ —

What COVID-19 precautions are in place?

We follow all local health guidelines, including regular sanitization, providing hand sanitizers, and maintaining social distancing in workout areas.
